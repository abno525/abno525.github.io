One thing that is config specific. I had to enable rsa logging in, as it was disabled by default. To do so, add to ~/.ssh/config:

Host *
    PubkeyAcceptedKeyTypes=+ssh-rsa
    HostKeyAlgorithms=+ssh-rsa
